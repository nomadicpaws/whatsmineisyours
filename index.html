Our mission

Good stuff belongs in the community, not the landfill.

What's Mine is Yours keeps good, usable things out of the landfill and back into the hands of neighbors who need them. We pick up brush, bulk items, and things people are throwing out — then sort, clean up, and resell or rehome them at little to no cost. Every pickup is a small act of care for our planet, our neighbors, and each other.

Saving money. Helping people. Helping our planet.
How it works

Four stops, one route.

From curb to new home — here's what happens after you call.

01
Pick up
Call or text Shane. We'll swing by and haul away your brush, bulk items, and unwanted things — free.
02
Sort & restore
Back at the warehouse, everything gets sorted. What's still good gets cleaned up and made ready for a new home.
03
Reuse & resell
Good stuff goes back out at little to no cost — through Facebook Marketplace and around the community.
04
Rehome, together
Proceeds and goods go back into the neighborhood — supporting local jobs and keeping resources close to home.

— People
